紋身是永久的。

紋身作品集也可以是永久的

Gogo Tattoo 使用區塊鏈科技創建一個牢不可破的永久的檔案，為紋身藝術家證明創意所有權，也為擁有此紋身的人記錄下此紋身的寓意。

我們的分散的平臺提供藝術家們機會去展示他們紋身才能，世界各地的紋身愛好者的科技和當地的紋身工作室的水准完全透明化，為客戶的利益和創作者的版權，提供了前所未有的保證。每一次紋身都可以是一次不留遺憾的體驗。

Gogo Tattoo 為什麼很酷？

幫助藝術家脫穎而出

紋身藝術家應該得到認可，並把他們的設計紋在最欣賞他們作品的人身上。我們的對等數位連接區塊鏈科技可以把藝術家和全球範圍內的潛在客戶以無法想像的管道相聯結。

讓紋身無憂

紋身是永久的，沒有人會想要一個永久的遺憾。我們被區塊鏈所擔保的市場允許客戶瀏覽藝術家的真實檔案，我們有絕對的信心，承諾客戶所選擇的藝術家想展現的是可以被傳達的。

使紋身店保持興隆

讓紋身店成為一個讓更多客戶遇見優秀紋身藝術家的地方，並讓紋身店的資料頁面在區塊鏈技術支援的網絡內陞級。當您的店需要新鮮的人才時，她/他總是可以在 Gogo Tattoo 找到你。

讓我們一步一脚印

在過去幾個月裏，在區塊鏈的世界裏發生了許多令人興奮的新聞，這些迷人又可怕的新聞吸引了我們的注意。智慧合約正在蓬勃發展，這一强大的新機制正獲得更廣泛的關注，但同時此機制的效能問題造成了一些諸如以太坊區塊鏈的最新分支之類的讓人緊張不安問題。我們所有的人都在密切注視。

針對上述情况，gogo.tattoo正在密切監測在對等網絡生態系統中的大環境，並繼續前進與發展。我們覺得更重要的是要用長遠的眼光看前面的路，而不是不顧一切的輕率開始行動。我們正在進入一個還沒有被科技人員知根知底的全新領域—讓想法變成現實的區塊鏈。

我們很高興我們正在建設發展中，其中的項目包括建設我們的紋身藝術家簡介頁，在那裡，你將能够將你的工作作為去中心化的一部分展示。這是世界各地的藝術家和紋身愛好者的網絡。我們非常期待能為您帶來這一切。我們一如既往希望有志同道合的人可以一起合作。

明年的我們會更酷

如果你在 gogo.tattoo 今年推出以來一直在關注我們，你可能會有一些關於如果將我們使命變成焦點的想法意見。在一方面，建立開發的這個將紋身經驗與充滿可能性的區塊鏈相連接的平臺是首要任務；另一方面，我們正在努力的線上下紋身世界中讓大家瞭解到我們的存在，我們從與當地紋身工作室和紋身藝術家交流開始，讓他們意識到我們正在朝著什麼樣方向發展.。

最近的最新消息總是令人興奮的，無論你最關心的是紋身還是網頁內部科技（兩者都會被永久保存）。我們在一個令人興奮的都市裏的一個秘密地點內工作。這個城市裏聚集著區塊鏈世界裏的主要工作者，大家都在此科技領域裏投資了自己的時間和精力，專注於這項可以讓互聯網世界改革的項目。再加上在區塊鏈圈子裏我們十分有前途的關注度，還有我們從那些從事紋身事業的人那裡得到的各種歡迎，這些都推動著我們前進，以一種無法預期的超快速度逐步將我們的願景變成現實。這個月我們現場發佈了一則紋身項目的工作證明，對於我們來說這是一個重要的里程碑。這可令人非常興奮，這個經驗繼續揭示了 gogo.tattoo 可以使紋身藝術家受益，也能使選擇了他們的客戶們受益，並讓允許他們進行紋身的工作室也受益。我們一直都知道我們能為人們做些什麼，但這意味著我們也需要接收更多人的迴響。

我們很高興能與眾多在幕後對我們的項目充滿熱情的人們一起步入新的一年。這是我們幫助著推動的一個短暫的，快速的，野蠻生長的旅程。接下去將會發生的事將會是驚奇的，是驚喜的，也是帶有啟發性的。我們期待著在新的一年裏我們繼續在這個創意世界的酷炫一角繼續一起改變世界。

祝好

Starting a busy year

The new year finds us introducing a bunch of new features as we continue documenting our artists’ awesome new tattoos and researching our way forward. On the tech side, there are quite a few attractive new features on our site. First, we made our announcement posts separate pages with their own links:

  • gogo.tattoo/post/2016-11-27-vision
  • gogo.tattoo/post/2016-12-17-annual-summary

Meanwhile tattoo artists’ personal pages are looking better and better all the time. We think the Year of the Rooster is a great occasion to update our community on what’s been going on and where we are looking next by introducing some links to our latest features.

Tags are up

Tattoos are now tagged so visitors can find and follow artists based on their interests, making it easier for artists to reach the right audiences with their expertise and work history. Artists’ profile pages will show their tags right at the top of the page, and tattoo pages will show tags just below the image.

Going forward visitors on gogo.tattoo will have an easy way of finding the artists who specialize in the kinds of designs they’re looking for, bringing us a huge step closer to realizing the transparent, human marketplace we envision gogo.tattoo becoming.

  • gogo.tattoo/gogo/tags/dragon
  • gogo.tattoo/aid/tags/color
  • gogo.tattoo/gogo/tags/watercolor
  • //gogo/tattoo/anchored_infinity_moon

New Design section: Link for ink

Another way we’re helping connect great artists and the people who will enjoy their work is our new design section. It’s the place to showcase the work you’re looking to make next and get recognition for your creativity.

  • gogo.tattoo/gogo/design

Github repository is alive

For programmers and other collaborators looking to get involved at gogo.tattoo, our github repo is filling up with ongoing updates on our progress and development. The demo/alpha version of the artists’ page is now open sourced.

Gogo-upload

Gogo-upload is an utility written in golang. It aims to add labeled watermarks, resize photos and upload the edited files to IPFS. It is half ready. We invite you to contibute.

May Update

A lot’s been happening at gogo.tattoo these past few months, giving us plenty of updates to report as we ease into Labor Day weekend. Open sourced backend/frontend development continues to drive our evolution as a platform in exciting directions, while our core group of artists working at the studio level has grown with the recent addition of tattoo master Xizi.

  • github.com/gogotattoo
  • gogo.tattoo/xizi

One development after another, our vision of a seamless interface between artists, clients, studios, and the individual tattoo sessions we’re working to document is taking form at gogo.tattoo.

On the mobile side, we’re hard at work on the alpha version of our open-sourced Android app.

  • app.gogo.tattoo
  • github.com/gogotattoo/gogo-android

It’s now approved on GooglePlay, so please get in contact if you’re interested in being an alpha tester! Adding to the excitement, the gogo.tattoo iOS app is also now in active development.

  • github.com/gogotattoo/gogo-ios

New site features include automatic watermarking for posted images and artist page localization, supporting our goal of offering talented artists visibility to international audiences beyond the regions where they are based. You can now view our artists’ pages in Russian and Chinese, as well as English. Meanwhile we’ve launched a page dedicated to the incredible studio that’s been at the heart of the action so far, downtown Shanghai’s Chu Shang Feng. To see where we’ve been documenting so much of the work on our site you can now visit this link:

  • gogo.tattoo/@chushangfeng

On the horizon, we are getting ready to launch tattube, a decentralized youtube for tattoos hosted on the InterPlanetary File System.

  • github.com/gogotattoo/ipfstube

It’s all happening fast now and we’re anticipating big news in the months ahead. Along with Twitter you can now follow us on Steemit, where you can hear more about our talented artists and the works they’ve been doing with us.

Part of our team, including Yana Gogo herself, are heading to Russia, where she and Valentin are goint to participate in the Saint-Petersburg Tattoo Weekend. The event will take place from May 12 to May 14.


Come to find us there!

The auction on Yana’s works is still in ongoing stage, so please contact us as soon as possible if you want to discuss designs which we could deliver at the event.
